Better Modelling of Baseball¶

Shouldn't baseball players have individual batting averages?

First attempt, unpooled:

  • don't share information amongst the players
  • no prior on individual players' batting averages
In [16]:
unpooled

Bayesian attempt, partially pooled:

  • do share information amongst the baseball players
  • yes prior on individual players' batting averages
  • $\Rightarrow$ regression towards the mean
  • AKA shrinkage, regularization, penalized
